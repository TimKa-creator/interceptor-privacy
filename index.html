Privacy Policy — Interceptor

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Interceptor ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Sofi Studio. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, why, and how you can control or delete it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Nickname (callsign) — required to create an account and appear on the leaderboard. Chosen by you, not personally identifying by itself.
  • Email address — optional. Used only to let you recover your account and sign in on another device. You can play and keep your account without providing an email.
  • Gameplay data — your in-game progress (coins, unlocked planes, cleared levels, endless-mode survival times) is stored so it can sync across devices when you are signed in.
  • Leaderboard scores — your nickname and best survival time per aircraft are shown publicly on the in-game leaderboard.

We do not collect your name, location, contacts, photos, or any device identifiers beyond what is needed for basic app operation.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To save and restore your game progress across devices.
  • To display leaderboard rankings.
  • To send a one-time verification code to your email, if you choose to link one.

We do not use your data for advertising profiling, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following processors to operate the app's account and leaderboard features:

  • Supabase (database, authentication, and server functions) — stores your account, nickname, email (if provided), and game-progress data.
  • Mailjet (email delivery) — sends the verification code email if you link an email address. Mailjet only receives your email address for this purpose.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy safeguards. All data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account (see below), your profile, saved progress, and leaderboard entries are permanently removed.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In-app: open Settings → Account → Delete Account and confirm. This immediately and permanently deletes your account, nickname, saved progress, and leaderboard entries.
  • Without the app installed: email us at s.o.f.i.studios2026@gmail.com with the nickname or email address associated with your account and request deletion. We will process the request within a reasonable time.

6. Children's Privacy

Interceptor is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Email is optional and not required to play.

7. Advertising and In-App Purchases

The app currently does not display third-party advertising or process real in-app purchases. If these features are enabled in a future update, this policy will be updated in advance to describe the additional data collected (e.g. by an ad network or payment processor).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: s.o.f.i.studios2026@gmail.com
